Collaborating with EG competition finalists to develop a comprehensive framework that centers children's agency in AI system design.

This ongoing project brings together competition finalists with our research team to create actionable guidelines for designers and developers working on AI systems for children. The framework addresses key dimensions of agency including autonomy, understanding, participation, and control.

The collaboration aims to bridge the gap between theoretical principles and practical implementation, providing concrete tools and methods that practitioners can use in their daily work.
