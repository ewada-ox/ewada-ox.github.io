Bringing together global leaders, researchers, and practitioners to address the critical challenges of AI design for the next generation.

The Generation AI Summit is a landmark event that convenes thought leaders from academia, industry, government, and civil society to discuss the future of AI as it relates to children and young people. The summit features keynote addresses, panel discussions, interactive workshops, and networking opportunities.

Topics include ethical AI design principles, regulatory frameworks, educational technology, digital rights for children, and the role of parents and educators in an AI-driven world. The summit aims to produce actionable recommendations and foster lasting collaborations across sectors.
