On November 11, 2025, Sir Nigel reflected on another year of seismic shifts and changes in the world of data, AI and technology more broadly [in the ODI summit](https://theodi.org/news-and-events/events/sir-nigel-shadbolt-state-of-the-data-nation/).


The event was chaired by [Emma Thwaites](https://theodi.org/profile/emma-thwaites/), Director of Global Policy and Corporate Affairs, ODI who has over 30 years of strategic business experience, in senior roles at the BBC and UK Cabinet Office, and founding and running a successful consultancy practice.
