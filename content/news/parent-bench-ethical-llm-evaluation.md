Launching Parent Bench, a framework for evaluating large language models designed to support parents in their digital parenting journey ethically and effectively.

Parent Bench provides a systematic approach to assessing how well LLMs support parents in navigating the complex landscape of children's digital lives. The framework evaluates models across dimensions including accuracy, cultural sensitivity, age-appropriateness of advice, and alignment with established child development principles.

This project addresses the growing reliance on AI tools for parenting advice, ensuring that these systems provide guidance that is both helpful and ethically sound. The evaluation framework is designed to be adaptable across different cultural contexts and parenting philosophies.
