Dr. Jun Zhao discusses the concept of agency in AI design for children, exploring how technology can empower rather than diminish children's autonomy and decision-making capabilities.

In this interview with Fam, Dr. Zhao shares insights from years of research at the Oxford Child-Centred AI Design Lab, highlighting the importance of designing AI systems that respect and promote children's agency. The discussion covers practical approaches to ensuring that children remain active participants rather than passive recipients of AI-driven experiences.

Key topics include the balance between protection and empowerment, age-appropriate design strategies, and the role of parents and educators in supporting children's digital autonomy.
