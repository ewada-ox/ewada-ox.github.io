Contributing to OECD's measurement of children's digital wellbeing and UNICEF's assessment of digital parent technology.

Our team is working with the OECD to develop robust metrics and measurement frameworks for assessing children's digital wellbeing across member countries. This work will inform policy recommendations and help governments track progress in creating safer, more beneficial digital environments for children.

Simultaneously, we are collaborating with UNICEF on a comprehensive assessment of digital parent technology, evaluating how these tools support or hinder effective digital parenting practices. The assessment will provide guidance for parents, developers, and policymakers alike.
