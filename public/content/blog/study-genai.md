We've launched a new study to explore how children interact with and make sense of generative AI tools. The study aims to:

- Understand how children interact with generative AI
- Understand when children would and wouldn't use generative AI

To get involved, send an email to oxfordccai[at]cs.ox.ac.uk with the subject line PRISM.
