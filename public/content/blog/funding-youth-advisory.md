We are excited to announce funding has been awarded to establish a youth advisory group focused on AI and data science projects at Oxford. The project is part of the Public and Community Engagement with Research Fellowship.

This group will:

- Bring young people's perspectives into AI research
- Co-design research materials
- Ensure research is relevant, responsible, and impactful
